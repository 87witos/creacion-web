BluDragon Software is an fictional indie video game company headquartered in Granada (Spain). It is the fith gaming company in Spain and the second market capitalization after Fake Games Company.


Founded and incorporated on 2021, by Edix student Pablo Hitos, the company was a pioneer of the early indie game industry in Granada (Spain) and promoted the designers and programmers responsible for its games as "software artists." BluDragon Software published numerous games and some productivity software for personal computers and smartphones, all of which were developed by external individuals or groups.


BluDragon Software also owns and operates major gaming studios such as RedDragon Software in Madrid, BDS in Barcelona, BDS Italy in Rome, RedFlying Studios in Stockholm and Los Angeles, Shadow Knight Studios in Edmonton and Austin, and Game is not Over entertainment in Los Angeles and Vancouver.
